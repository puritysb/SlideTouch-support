Features

Everything runs on your Mac. No cloud. No subscription. No extra cost.

01

On-Device Text Detection

SlideTouch uses Apple's Vision framework to detect and recognize text in image-based slides. Text regions are identified with precise bounding boxes, and you can click any detected text to start editing. All processing happens locally on your Mac's Neural Engine. Supports English, Korean, and Japanese.

02

Seamless Text Replacement

When you edit text, the original characters are erased and the background is restored automatically. The LaMa inpainting model runs entirely on your Mac's Neural Engine to fill in gradients, patterns, and photographs. Then your new text is rendered precisely in place.

03

Object Selection & Move

Draw a stroke over any element in a slide, and on-device segmentation generates a precise mask around it. Move, reposition, or remove the selected object entirely. The background behind it is seamlessly restored.

04

Non-Destructive Editing

Every edit in SlideTouch is a layer on top of the original image. Nothing is permanently changed until you export. Toggle, reorder, or delete any edit layer at any time, with full undo/redo support. Browse all pages in the sidebar.

05

Rich Text & Shapes

Full control over typography: choose fonts, adjust sizes, set colors, and align text. Apply different styles to individual text runs. Add annotations with rectangles, ellipses, arrows, and freeform curves.

06

Multi-Format Export

Export your edited slides as a new PDF document, individual PNG/JPEG images, a video slideshow, or an animated GIF. The original file is never modified.
